Why “Puff, Puff, Pass”?

The name is about progression. First, you track the puffs. Then, you understand the habit. Eventually, the goal is to pass on smoking, vaping, pouches, or whatever form of nicotine use you are working to move away from.

Research-backed encouragement.

Tracking can create awareness. Awareness can support behavior change. Puff, Puff, Pass uses daily tracking, motivational reinforcement, and habit reflection to help users better understand their nicotine patterns.

Track the Habit

Users can see when, where, and how often they use nicotine. This helps make patterns easier to spot and easier to address.

Daily Reinforcement

The app provides motivational messages and educational prompts designed to support consistency and build confidence.

Progress Over Perfection

Quitting or cutting back can be difficult. Puff, Puff, Pass is built to support small wins, honest tracking, and forward movement.
